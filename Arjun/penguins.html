Swimming

An emperor penguin is a bird that can not fly but do not get me wrong they are great swimmers. Emperor penguins will use their wings as fins instead of using them to fly like other birds. When emperor penguins swim they use countershading. Tye have white bellies which make them look like the ocean floor and their black are backs to look like the ocean surface. This helps them stay safe from their predators. Some of their predators are leopard and fur seals, sea lions, sharks and killer whales.

Life Cycle

The life cycle of an emperor penguin starts with the egg being laid. This is most likely to happen in May or June. Next after 65 days the egg hatches. Then the dad will go looking for food for 3 months while the mom takes care of the egg. Then the egg is sent to a group called creches. Then at the age of 19 it is an adult.
