Intro

So welcome to the Super Earasers article! You might think “Why are erasers super? They are super because they give you second chances! Unfortunately, easers are not given respect so we decided to change that. It has been an idea so now we are going to spread it to readers like you throughout the world. Come on with us as we go through the makings of eraser clothing,houses and jobs and even money!

Start up

In order to start your ‘super’ journey you need the following Three 8 x 11 in paper(8 x 11.5 is fine to) pieces of paper. 1.One eraser
2.$1000 eraser dollars.
3.You also need glue,tape, scissors and markers!
Now were should we start? What should we make first? Let us start with the eraser cover!
