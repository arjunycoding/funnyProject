The Big,Small and Traning tops

Their are two types of lego beys Big and small. They both still have about the same amount of peices but for the big beys they normally have a wider base and for the small tops they have well... a small base

Then the training beys are not to big nor to small they help both types.

The peices

Here are the peices that you should have: A string,A base,A tip and design. The string is the thing that holds everything together. It can look like a start shaped rod or a black clyinder rod. The base is basically the plate that balences your top. It should look circular. Then it is the tip. It helps your top spin. Then the design. It makes your top look snazzy!
